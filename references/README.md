# Academic References

This folder catalogues the references cited in the development of the **Compact Hexagonal Fractal Patch Antenna**.

---

## IEEE Style Citations

1.  O. Benkhadda, M. Saih, S. Ahmad, A. J. A. Al-Gburi, Z. Zakaria and Chaji, "A Miniaturized Tri-Wideband Sierpinski Hexagonal-Shaped Fractal Antenna for Wireless Communication Applications," *Fractal and Fractional*, vol. 7, no. 2, pp. 115, 2023.
2.  J. A. Jadhav and R. S. Pawase, "Design of Hexagonal Fractal Antenna Array for Multiband Wireless Application," *International Journal of Engineering Research and Technology (IJERT)*, vol. 4, no. 1, Jan. 2015.
3.  K. S. Waghmode, S. B. Deosarkar and P. K. Kadbe, "Hexagonal Fractal Antenna for 1.6 GHz GPS Application," *International Journal of Science and Research (IJSR)*, vol. 4, no. 2, pp. 1492–1494, Feb. 2015.
4.  M. Karhana and R. Kumar, "A Review on Fractal Antenna," *International Journal of Engineering Research & Technology (IJERT)*, 2017.
5.  S. N. A. Khan et al., "Advanced Design and Experimental Performance Analysis of a Hexagonal Fractal Antenna Array for 5G/6G Wireless Systems," *Spectrum of Engineering Sciences*, 2024.
6.  S. Kumar Dhakad, U. Dwivedi, S. Rawat, Y. Agarwal and A. Joshi, "A Slotted Microstrip Antenna with Fractal Design for Surveillance Radar Applications in X-Band," *International Journal of Engineering and Technology*, vol. 7, no. 3, pp. 64–67, 2018.
7.  R. Kumar, R. Sinha, A. Choubey and S. K. Mahto, "An Ultrawideband Monopole Antenna Using Hexagonal-Square Shaped Fractal Geometry," *Journal of Electromagnetic Waves and Applications*, vol. 35, no. 2, 2021.
8.  A. E. Journal Authors, "Analysis of a Miniaturized Hexagonal Sierpinski Gasket Fractal Microstrip Antenna for Modern Wireless Communications," *AEU – International Journal of Electronics and Communications*, vol. 123, 2020.
9.  A. A. Khudhair, S. Q. Jabbar and D. Wang, "Fractal Antennas (Study and Review)," *International Journal of Computers & Technology*, vol. 15, 2016.
10. C. A. Balanis, *Antenna Theory: Analysis and Design*, 3rd Edition, John Wiley & Sons, 2005.
11. R. C. Johnson and H. Jasik, *Antenna Engineering Handbook*, 2nd Edition, McGraw-Hill, 1984.
12. D. M. Pozar, *Microwave Engineering*, 4th Edition, Wiley, 2011.
